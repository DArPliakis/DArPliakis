# Demetrios A. Pliakis, PhD
**Mathematical Physicist & Applied Mathematician**  
*ADITAL LLC *

> **Bridging PDE Theory, Geometric Analysis, and Computational Algorithms**

### Expertise
- **PDEs & Nonlinear Diffusion**: Porous Medium Equation (PME), Reaction-PME (R-PME), Stefan conditions  
- **Geometric Analysis**: Laplacian eigenvalues on hypersurfaces, Hardy inequalities, level sets

- **Interdisciplinary**: Real estate modeling (gentrification, market dynamics), quantum/classical physics
